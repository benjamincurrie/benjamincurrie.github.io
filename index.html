I design, develop and create beautiful usable websites and applications for desktop and mobile.

Currently based in Montreal, Canada, with a Bachelor in Computer Science from the University of Western Australia and over 7 years experience designing and developing websites and applications around the world.

For a long time I have struggled to define what it is I am; Designer, Web Developer, PHP Programmer, <Coder>. So I've come up with a new term, Designoper.

De·sign·op·er /dəˈzīnəpər/ (Noun):

  1. A hybrid of a designer and developer. Incorporating both into their trade they develop beautiful code and create structured designs.
  2. An digital "jack of all trades".
  3. An online creator who can handle any task despite the challenges.

While not always easy to define, I love what I do because I am passionate about creating. And that’s what makes the difference as I believe when you are passionate about what you do, you produce higher quality work.

Booking.com

Messaging Platform

Clear communication between Booking.com's accommodation partners and their guests is paramount for a successful stay. Prior to their trip a guest might want to communicate with someone in order to make a request or get clarity on a topic. The current way to do this was not ideal, either through email which was slow or via phone which meant the guest had no record of the conversation.

The Booking.com Messaging Platform was born out of a need for a quick communication tool between the guest and accommodation partner, it was required to be accessible across all devices so we designed, built and launched across both web and native apps at the same time.

Role Qualitative research, Quantitative research, Prototyping, App & Web design, Design strategy

BulkSMS

Promotional Website

BulkSMS is an Australian SMS service enabling users to easily send mass business communications and marketing texts. With a base of both business and marketing clients the website was designed to look clean and professional while being bold enough to stand out against the competition. Using the latest CSS technologies it is an extremely lightweight website, loading fast in both traditional and mobile browsers.

Role: Web Design & Implementation
Technologies: Photoshop, HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, PyroCMS

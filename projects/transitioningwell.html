Transitioning Well

Promotional Website

Transitioning Well is a service that aids business professionals in their transitions between carenthood, retirement and career changes.

The Transitioning Well team wanted a bold, fullscreen layout utilising modal popups to layer information on the page. It was a challenging concept but I was able to come up with a custom Wordpress theme that utilised shortcodes for the team to be able to add new buttons and modal popups without having to understand the complex markup that the theme needed.

Role: Web Design & Implementation
Technologies: Photoshop, HTML, CSS, Javascript

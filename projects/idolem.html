Idolem

Promotional Website

Idolem is a Hot Yoga franchise with five studios spread over Montreal, Canada.

My position was to create a mobile ready HTML5 Wordpress theme based on the design templates provided by Imagemotion. The focus of this design was the beautiful imagery created by our photo shoot, the full screen video on the home page and backgrounds on all following pages creates an immersive experience for web visitors.

Role: Custom Wordpress Theme
Contracted With: Imagemotion
Technologies: Photoshop, Wordpress, HTML5, CSS3, Javascript

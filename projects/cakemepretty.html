Cake Me Pretty

Ecommerce • Promotional website

Cake Me Pretty is a boutique patisserie based in Montreal, Canada. They invisioned streamlining the cake design process by allowing customers to build cakes directly on their website.

My task was to create a custom Wordpress theme based on the design files provided by Imagemotion and to develop a Woocommerce plugin that allows customers to design their own cake and order them directly on the website.

Role: Custom Wordpress Theme, Woocommerce Development
Contracted With: Imagemotion
Technologies: Photoshop, Wordpress, Wocommerce HTML5, CSS3, Javascript
